Assetto Corsa

Assetto Corsa is a highly realistic sim racing game developed by Kunos Simulazioni. It was first released on December 19, 2014 for PC, with console versions launching in August 2016. The game is known for its accurate physics, laser-scanned tracks, and extensive modding support.

Available Platforms

  • Microsoft Windows (PC)
  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox One
  • iOS (as of August 2021 - though this version of the game isn't fully released still)

Assetto Corsa Competizione

Assetto Corsa Competizione is the official GT World Challenge video game and the successor to Assetto Corsa. It was released on May 29, 2019 for PC, followed by PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in June 2020, and PlayStation 5 and Xbox Series X/S in February 2022.

The game focuses on GT3, GT4, and other racing classes, offering an immersive experience with dynamic weather, day-night cycles, and realistic handling powered by Unreal Engine 4.

Available Platforms

  • Microsoft Windows (PC)
  • PlayStation 4
  • Xbox One
  • PlayStation 5
  • Xbox Series X/S
