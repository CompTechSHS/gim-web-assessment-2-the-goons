the binding of isaac is a game created by edmund mcmillen (you might know him from super meat boy or something) ... it's a roguelike game based on the original legend of zelda's dungeons . the premise of the game is that you play as isaac and you shoot enemies with your tears and you can collect items to benefit your playthrough.. the game is Infinitely replayable as the main gameplay loop is based on runs where you play a new instance of the game every time you start a new run...
