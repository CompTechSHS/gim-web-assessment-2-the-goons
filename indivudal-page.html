Grand Theft Auto V

Grand Theft Auto V (GTA V) is an open-world action-adventure game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was first released on September 17, 2013. Set in the fictional state of San Andreas, the game follows three protagonists as they commit daring heists and navigate a vibrant criminal underworld.
