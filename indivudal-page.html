The GTA Page - By George

Grand Theft Auto 5 is an open world action game developed by Rockstar North and published by Rockstar Games. It was first released on September 17, 2013. Set in the state of Los Santos, the game follows three main charaters as they commit heists and navigate a criminal underworld.
